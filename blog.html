Impact of Technology in Daily Life and Business


Technology is the medium of evolution of the current mordern world. Everything in the world currently is shaped up by the advancement in technology. The development in technological sector increases the proficiency and efficiency of the other sectors by tenfolds. The evolution in technology has revolutionized the future of entertainment, education, health, communication and many more of the activities that we couldn't even fathom.

The development in technological aspect of human beings has enabled us to reach from moon to interstellar galaxy in search of the unknown. Many answers related to the physics and mathematics that lied beneath the curtains of the discovery has been rediscovered by lifting the curtains with the wheels of technological advancement. There were diseases that were fathomed to be uncurable that have been cured by the technological advancement in health sector. Similarly, the supply of the mass produced medication have only enabled us humans to live in prosperity.

The technological development has impacted a great deal of business and industrial sector. There has been surge in use of different analytical tools, artificial intelligence, different management tools etc that have been greatly influencing the proficiency of the business market. Even the markets are being analyzed through the use of technology. There have been use of techonology in field of every industry imaginable. From small independent artists to corporate giants, they all use some sort of software products that leverage their workload and efficiency.
Similarly, the household has been provided with efficient helping hand tools that only provide comfort to the people. Like groceries, medical bills, electrical bills, to entertainment aspects, there have been many technology with or without integration of the sophisticated software technology. Just with a tap of finger tip all of the services, EVEN the switching on and off of the lights is achieved through technology.

In conclusion there have been huge influence of technology directly in every single aspect of humanity. The question is whether or not this dependacny will be fruitful or will be the cause of extinction and doevolution of human society. We can ponder about it. We ought to look the future of humanity from a positive outlook but who is to say what can happen far off in the future. All we can do is hope
